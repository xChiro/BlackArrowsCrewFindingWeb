How to Use

Citizen Crew Finding is a web application that allows you to easily find other players to play Star Citizen with. Here’s how you can use it:

Step 1: Log in using your Discord account

Use your Discord credentials to easily sign in. This allows you to seamlessly connect with other players in Star Citizen.

Step 2: Create or Join a Crew

You can create a new crew or join an existing one with just a few clicks. It’s easy to start playing with others!

Step 3: Communicate via Discord

Once you’ve joined a crew, a Discord channel is automatically generated for you to communicate with your teammates.
